How to Play DTA 6 ?

Grand Theft Auto became a hit because it let people run around a world similar to the real world and do whatever chaotic thing they wanted, so we have games like DTA 6. This world is not like ours and probably came directly from the internet. The people are geometric ragdolls that speak in memes and you're the only guy with full control over his limbs. How you decide to use that control is up to you, but the city is your playground and you have the means to get a weapon arsenal to play with even more explosive results.
